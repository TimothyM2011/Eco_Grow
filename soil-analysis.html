NPK Sensor for Soil Nutrient Monitoring

The NPK sensor monitors levels of nitrogen, phosphorus, and potassium in the soil. This data helps determine soil fertility and optimize plant growth.

In addition to enhancing crop yields, the NPK sensor also contributes to sustainable bioenergy production, supporting renewable energy efforts in agriculture.
