The Energy Gap in Agriculture

In remote agricultural regions, up to 40% of farms lack reliable and sustainable energy sources. This shortage hinders productivity and increases operating costs by up to 30%.

Our project addresses these issues, aiming to fulfill Sustainable Development Goals 7 (Affordable and Clean Energy) and 13 (Climate Action).
