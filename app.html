EcoGrow App: Smart Monitoring and Notifications

The EcoGrow App provides real-time data on soil conditions, nutrient levels, and irrigation needs. By utilizing IoT sensors and SMFCs, the app offers continuous monitoring, empowering farmers with data-driven insights for optimal crop health and efficient resource management.

Features include customizable notifications, remote access, and data analytics for effective farming solutions.
