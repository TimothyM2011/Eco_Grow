EcoGrow Systems Solution

EcoGrow Systems provides a multi-faceted solution using Soil Microbial Fuel Cells (SMFCs) to generate bioenergy and NPK sensors for continuous soil nutrient monitoring.

NPK Soil Monitoring

Our sensors monitor nitrogen, phosphorus, and potassium levels in the soil, optimizing plant growth and enabling bioenergy production.

SMFCs and Continuous Bioenergy Generation

SMFCs generate clean, renewable electricity using soil microbes, powering IoT sensors and the EcoGrow app for real-time monitoring.
